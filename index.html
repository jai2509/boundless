Our Vision

We believe that every girl has the right to access sanitary pads and education. We aim to provide free sanitary pads to college girls in need and promote menstrual hygiene awareness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of life for college girls who cannot afford sanitary pads. We achieve this by collecting donations and distributing sanitary pads to college girls in need.
